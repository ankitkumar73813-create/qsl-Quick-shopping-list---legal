Privacy Policy – QSL

Quick Shopping List & Productivity Hub

Introduction

Welcome to QSL – Quick Shopping List, an advanced offline-first Productivity Hub developed by ANKIT KUMAR from India. QSL is designed to streamline your daily life through a unified interface featuring Dynamic Shopping Lists, Calendar Integration, Global Scanner Pay, Price Calculator, Unit Converter, Quick Notes, Magic Import, and a high-precision Reminder System. By using this application, you acknowledge our commitment to providing a secure, high-quality, and all-in-one shopping assistant experience.

1. Offline-First Architecture

QSL is a strictly 'Offline-First' application. Your Shopping Lists, Quick Notes, Expenses, and Reminders are stored only on your device. We do not use servers or cloud sync, ensuring you have full control over your data stored on your device.

2. Core Application Features & Functional Purpose

QSL is a comprehensive productivity hub designed to simplify your shopping experience. Our core features include:

  • Home Screen List Management: Effortlessly create, organize, and manage multiple shopping lists with real-time progress tracking.
  • Calendar Integration: A specialized calendar feature that allows users to view, plan, and manage their shopping tasks and reminders in a chronological layout, ensuring long-term organization.
  • Global Scanner Pay & QR Utility: A smart QR scanner to instantly process payment codes, product links, or contact details, making checkout faster and safer.
  • Advanced Utility Toolbar: Includes a professional Unit Converter (for weight/volume/length), Quick Notes (for instant thought capture), and a precision Price Calculator for on-the-go shopping calculations.
  • Magic Import: A unique feature to quickly bring in external data or lists into the app without manual typing.
  • Precision Reminder System: To prevent users from missing critical shopping tasks or time-sensitive errands, QSL provides a high-reliability reminder engine. This system ensures that users are alerted exactly when needed, which is a fundamental part of the app's utility as a shopping assistant.

3. No Data Collection or Sharing

QSL does not collect, store, or share any personal information (such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or locations) with our servers or any third parties. Your data is yours alone and never leaves your device. Since QSL is an offline-first application, no data is transmitted over the internet. Any data interaction within the device follows standard Android security protocols and encryption.

4. Full Permission Transparency (9 Core Disclosures)

To comply with global privacy standards and ensure full transparency, here is why QSL requires these 9 specific permissions based on its features:

  • 1. Camera (CAMERA): Used exclusively for the 'Global Scanner Pay' and QR utility to process payment codes or product links. The app does not capture, store, or transmit any images or videos.
  • 2. Post-Reboot Recovery (RECEIVE BOOT COMPLETED): Ensures that your active shopping reminders are not lost if your device restarts. This permission allows the app to automatically reschedule your tasks to maintain reliability.
  • 3. Haptic Feedback (VIBRATE): Provides physical notification alerts, ensuring you are aware of urgent reminders even when the device is in a pocket or in a noisy environment.
  • 4. User-Initiated Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE EXACT ALARM): This is a User-Initiated permission. It allows the app to trigger a notification at the precise second you specify for a shopping task, preventing any system-induced delays in time-sensitive reminders.
  • 5. Time-Critical Reminder Precision (USE EXACT ALARM): As a productivity tool, QSL relies on this permission to ensure its core reminder engine delivers alerts exactly when needed. This is strictly 'User-Initiated' and is only active when a user manually schedules a high-precision alert.
  • 6. Visual Notifications (POST NOTIFICATIONS): Required for Android 13+ to display reminder banners and status bar icons, which is the primary way the app communicates scheduled tasks to you.
  • 7. Notification Delivery Assurance (REQUEST IGNORE BATTERY OPTIMIZATIONS): Used to ensure that the Android system's battery-saving features do not silence or delay your scheduled shopping reminders, ensuring 100% alert reliability.
  • 8. Lock Screen Visibility (USE FULL SCREEN INTENT): Allows the app to display high-priority reminders as a full-screen alert even when your phone is locked, ensuring you don't miss critical tasks while shopping.
  • 9. Connectivity & Security (INTERNET & QUERIES): Internet is limited to opening external help links. Queries are used specifically to interact with verified payment or productivity apps already installed on your device to complete user-initiated actions.

5. No Third-Party Tracking & Libraries

We maintain a 'Zero-Analytics' environment. No Google Analytics, No Ads, and No tracking SDKs are present. Note: The application may use the Google Fonts API to load external fonts to provide a better user interface, which is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

6. Data Retention & Deletion Policy

QSL is an account-free application. Since no user account is ever created, all your data (Shopping Lists, Notes, and Expenses) is stored exclusively on your device. Please note: Uninstalling or deleting the application will immediately and permanently erase all your data. Because we do not use cloud storage, this data cannot be recovered once the app is removed.

7. Children's Privacy

QSL does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is local, parents can delete the app to remove all information instantly.

8. Global Privacy Compliance (GDPR & CCPA)

Although QSL is an offline app and does not 'collect' data in the traditional sense, we respect global standards:

• GDPR (Europe): You have the right to access, export, and delete your data. Since all data is local, you can exercise these rights by using the app's export/delete features or uninstalling the app.

• CCPA (California): We do not sell your personal information. In fact, we do not have access to your data.

This Privacy Policy and your use of the application are governed by the laws of India. Any disputes arising will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in India.
